You’ve told us that the solitaire game is not working as you can’t drag a card to where you want it.

Here’s how to make a move in this version of the game:

To move a card click on the card you want to move. The edges will turn yellow. Next click on the space where you want it to go.

The game can be a little slow to register a move so give it time for the move to happen.

Click on the pile in the top left of the game to reveal new cards.

Double-clicking a card will place it on the relevant stack if possible, for example, if you double-click on an Ace it will automatically move it across to the completed stack.
